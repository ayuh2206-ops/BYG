Cookie Policy

We value your privacy as much as your journey. Here’s how we use cookies to make your travel planning smoother and more personalized.

Last updated: October 24, 2023

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site. Think of them as a memory for the website, allowing it to recognize you and remember your preferences like your selected currency or past destination searches.

We use cookies to enhance your experience on Before You Go, ensuring that your travel planning process is seamless. Some cookies are essential for the technical operation of our website, while others help us to improve our service and deliver relevant content to you.
