About the Tour

Immerse yourself in the spiritual heart of Bali with this curated 7-day journey. From the mist-covered rice terraces of Ubud to the sacred waters of Tirta Empul, every moment is designed to rejuvenate your spirit.

The highlight of the trip includes a sunrise trek up Mount Batur, offering panoramic views that define the island's volcanic beauty. Coupled with daily yoga sessions and authentic culinary experiences, this isn't just a holiday; it's a reset for your soul.
